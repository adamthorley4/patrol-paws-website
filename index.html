About Renata

Dog Walking With a Personal Touch

"Hi, I'm Renata, founder of Patrol Paws."

After years of working with dogs and serving as a police officer, I created Patrol Paws to provide the kind of care I'd want for my own dog, Bea.

My background in law enforcement gave me discipline, reliability, and a deep understanding of trust — values I carry into every single walk. Combined with my university studies in animal behaviour and hands-on experience with all breeds, sizes, and temperaments, I'm uniquely placed to give your dog the care they truly deserve.

I've also spent time helping stray dogs during travels abroad, which cemented something I already knew: every dog deserves exercise, enrichment, patience, and genuine love — not just a quick lap around the block.
